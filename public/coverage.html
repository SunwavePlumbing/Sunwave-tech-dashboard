Paid Jobs Audit

Compares every paid invoice in Housecall Pro to what shows up on the KPI dashboard. Some gaps are normal — work done one month often gets paid the next, so the totals won't always match.

Enter the diagnostics password and pick a period. The tool will enumerate every paid invoice in that window from Housecall Pro, cross-reference each against the current technician leaderboard, and surface any invoice whose dollars didn’t make it onto the dashboard.
